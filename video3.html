Elemental | All Clips From The Movie

"Elemental" is a heartwarming animated movie about a fire girl named Ember and a water boy named Wade who come from different worlds. As they explore Element City, they learn to understand each other and show that even opposites can be friends. The film teaches us about acceptance, love, and respecting differences.
