BOUNTY | Animated Short Film

"Bounty" is a touching animated short film about a brave girl and a mysterious robot who meet on a desolate planet. Though they seem completely different at first, they form a strong bond as they journey through a harsh and lonely world. Together, they discover the power of trust, friendship, and hope. The film shows us that even in the most unlikely places, connection and kindness can grow.
