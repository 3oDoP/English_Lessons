Dora's Night Light Adventure

“Dora's Night Light Adventure” is a short animated episode where Dora and her friend Boots go on a nighttime journey to find a night light. Along the way, they use a map, sing songs, and face challenges like avoiding Swiper. The episode helps young learners improve listening and vocabulary through simple language and visual storytelling.
